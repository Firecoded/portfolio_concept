About Me

 As long as I can remember I've always been fascinated with how things were created. This curiosity led me to become a computer technician and I spent many years improving my craft. I was able to disassemble complicated machines and help others understand how they worked. I took on many roles within companies but always found satisfaction in finding out why something worked or why it didn't work.

 My curiosity led me to dive into how lines of code interacted with hardware to deliver a user experience. Changing someone elses code was not enough for me, I wanted to create. I focused my attention and spent thousands of hours learning and practicing. I have since embarked on a journey to never stop learning and creating. I look forward to the next project and I love to discuss any topic related to technology or programming, feel free to reach out.
